Namaz Timings

Fajr

“O you who have believed, seek help through patience and prayer. Indeed, Allah is with the patient.”

Dhuhr

Take care of what is between you and Allah, and he will take care of what is between you and the people.”

Asr

“Increase in worship before your responsibilities increase. Then you won't have time to worship as much.”

Maghrib

"Certainly will the believers have succeeded. They who are during their prayer humbly submissive. And they who turn away from ill speech."

Isha

“Our Lord! Forgive me and my parents, and (all) the believers on the Day when the reckoning will be establishe.”

Jumma

"The Prophet (saw) said, Straighten your rows as the straightening of rows is essential for a perfect and correct prayer."
